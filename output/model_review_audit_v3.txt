--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. ECONOMETRIC SPECIFICATION (econometrics-modeling)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1) Estimand
   - Reduced-form VAR( p ) on transformed series; structural objects via Cholesky,
     sign restrictions on impact (h=0), or VECM long-run relations.

2) Identification assumptions
   - Cholesky: ordering and small-open-economy exogeneity of external block.
   - Sign restrictions: set identification; columns of A0 = P @ Q assigned to
     named shocks in **table order** (see docstring in sign_restriction_irfs).
   - VECM: cointegration rank chosen from Johansen — sensitive to sample and
     deterministic term (ci).

3) Threats (explicit)
   - China: pipeline uses **nominal** quarterly GDP YoY from FRED unless user
     replaces with real growth — confounds real vs price effects (MEDIUM for
     interpretation of "China growth shock").
   - CPI: **interpolated** from annual World Bank where spline used — smooths
     high-frequency variation (HIGH for CPI equation; priority for manual download).
   - Sign restrictions on **transformed** (e.g. differenced) variables: signs are
     on innovations in the estimated model, not always on published levels (MEDIUM).
   - Residual autocorrelation / structural breaks: TVP-VAR and subsample checks
     mitigate but do not replace explicit break dummies (documented limitation).

B. MATHEMATICAL CORRECTNESS (model-review-math-econ)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Issue M1 [LOW] — VECMResultsWrapper.aic/bic
   - Formula uses Gaussian log-likelihood with det(Sigma_u) and ad-hoc k_params;
   - Not identical to statsmodels VECM information criteria.
   - Impact: model comparison labels only; primary inference uses statsmodels VECM fit.

Issue M2 [LOW] — TVP-VAR Kalman / forgetting
   - Simplified equation-by-equation filter; not a full TVP-VAR posterior.
   - Impact: exploratory time-varying paths; do not treat as fully Bayesian TVP-VAR.

Issue M3 [VERIFIED] — FEVD row sums
   - fevd[h,i,:] sums to 1 by construction (cum_sq / row sum).
   - Tests added for numerical stability.

Issue M4 [MEDIUM] — Sign restriction matrix mapping
   - Restrictions apply to Theta_h[vi, si] with si = 0..n_shocks-1 only if
     n_shocks <= k; only first len(sign_table) columns are tested.
   - Economic labels attach to **column index**, not to a uniquely identified
     structural shock without additional ordering restrictions (standard in set
     identification — disclose in paper).
