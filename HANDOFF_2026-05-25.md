## Critical Things Not To Get Wrong

1. **Do NOT run MCMC convergence diagnostics** on the BVAR. The Minnesota prior with fixed Σ draws analytically. Trace plots, ESS, R-hat are all meaningless category errors. See Lesson 18.

2. **Do NOT use `hk_property_price_qoq` in VECM**. It is I(0) by construction. VECM needs I(1) level series. Use `hk_property_price_idx` from `data/hk_property_price_rvd_quarterly.csv`.

3. **Do NOT extend the sample pre-1998**. Council verdict: keep 1998 Q1. HIBOR binding constraint + institutional regime change at 1997 handover. See Lesson 21. Paper paragraph already written in `paper/main.tex` Section 3.2.

4. **Do NOT touch the paper yet**. Paper is Phase N — held until Phase 10 synthesis is done. `paper/main.tex` exists but is stale for most sections.

5. **p=3 is degenerate** — optimizer returns pi1=0.01 (extremely tight, data ignored). Always use p=4. See Lesson 17.

6. **IRF indexing**: statsmodels and alexandria both start at h=0. Request h=9 from alexandria to get 9 points. No h-1 offset. See Lesson 19.

7. **FEVD HIBOR own-share (86%)** is not evidence HIBOR is self-driven — past FFR effects travel through lagged HIBOR terms and show up as own-share. This is a known VARX FEVD limitation. See Lesson 16.
