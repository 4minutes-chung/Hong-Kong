B) MODEL-REVIEW-MATH-ECON (formulas & mapping)
-----------------------------------------------
B1. FEVD from orthogonalized IRFs: FEVD_ij(h) = sum_s theta_ij,s^2 / sum_s sum_k theta_ik,s^2
    Implementation in compute_fevd() matches this definition (cumulative sum of squared IRF entries).

B2. Cholesky structural impact: Theta_h = Phi_h P with P = chol(Sigma_u). Consistent with standard VAR.

B3. Sign restrictions: Columns 0..n_shocks-1 of A0 = P Q are checked against sign_table rows.
    Joint restrictions on shock 1 and 2 columns — interpret as "first two structural shocks"
    satisfying named patterns; not separately rotating shock labels (standard partial ID).

B4. VECM -> VAR coefficients: Conversion from (alpha, beta, gamma) used for IRF/FEVD comparison.
    Severity: MEDIUM — companion matrix of VECM in levels can have eigenvalues on/near unit circle;
    long-horizon FEVD from this representation should be interpreted cautiously vs. reduced-form VAR.

B5. TVP-VAR (Kalman + forgetting): Heuristic state drift; not a full Bayesian TVP-VAR.
    Severity: LOW for exploratory plots; label as "illustrative time-varying OLS-Kalman" in paper if pushed.

B6. China GDP: If series is nominal GDP YoY, it confounds price and quantity. Severity: HIGH for
    interpreting "China growth shock" as real activity — mitigate with real data or explicit caveat.
